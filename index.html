Autonomous Rover

A four-wheel research rover that maps indoor spaces, plans collision-free routes, and holds its heading across uneven terrain.

  • ROS 2
  • C++
  • LiDAR
  • PID
+/- 2.1 cmpath accuracy

02BIOMECHATRONICS
BIO

Smart Prosthetic Hand

A tendon-driven, underactuated hand prototype that translates surface EMG signals into three reliable grip patterns.

  • Python
  • EMG
  • CAD
  • Prototyping
180 msresponse time
Build notes +

Iterated the finger geometry through six printed prototypes, trained a lightweight classifier, and reduced actuator count with a differential tendon system.

Wind Tunnel Test Rig

A compact instrumented test section for comparing lift, drag, and pressure distribution across student-built airfoils.

  • MATLAB
  • DAQ
  • CFD
  • FEA
4.8%CFD variance
Build notes +
